FEAR PONG

Please ask for the tablet at the bar.

Rules are similar to those in Beer Pong with the addition of the following:

Put a marker (that you should receive with the tablet) on each of your cups.

If your opponent lands the ball in your cup, press the 'Give me a dare' button. You will be granted a dare.

You now have a choice. Either do the dare and do not touch the cup, or you do not do the dare, and drink the cup.

If you did the dare, remove the marker from the cup. If your opponent lands the ball in that cup again, you have to drink it, no matter what.
